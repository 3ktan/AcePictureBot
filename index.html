WaifuRegister [args] / HusbandoRegister [args]

Register a waifu and/or a husbando!
Automaticaly recive an image every #WaifuWednesday or manually by using MyWaifu/MyHusbando.
Before using this command please read these things:
1. You MUST follow the bot to register!
2. Use their full name (If you're not sure check MyAnimeList)!
3. Don't include any special characters!
(ū > uu | ç > c | è > e)
4. Sometimes single names will have to have their show name in brackets "( )" (See "more examples" first)!
5. If the name is single and you're not too sure, the bot will automaticaly help you when registering!
6. You are limited at using the register command - 5 times per 300 hours (Click here to see why)!
7. Seasonal waifus are a bad idea as not much art is out there!
DO NOT REGISTER: a real person, anime name, your twitter handle, tags (1girl, 1boy, etc.), or a pony.

Frequently asked questions

Q: Why am I limited?
A: Limits are here for many reasons. I want every command (shipgirl, touhou, etc.) to always include an image. These image sites have their own limits, along side Twitter's API.

Q: What are the current limits?
A: Currently these are the limits:-
WaifuRegister: 5 Times x 300 Hours
MyWaifu: 5 Times x 3 Hours
Airing: 5 Times x 10 Hours
Every Other Command: 10 Times x 2 Hours
You are also limited on using: 5 Commands x 2 Minutes

Q: Why is MyWaifu limited less on WaifuWednesday?
A: The limit is lowered to 2 Times x 1 Hour while the "WaifuWednesday" job is running. The job runs at 8am (GMT) and runs for about 4+ hours. This is to limit the chances of errors for everyone. Once the job is finished it will go back to the orginal limits.
