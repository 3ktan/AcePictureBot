Automaticaly recive an image every #WaifuWednesday or manually by using MyWaifu/MyHusbando.
Before using this command please read these things:
1. Use their full name.
2. Make sure you spell their name correctly (this includes Å« > uu).
3. Sometimes sigle names will have to have their show name in () (see example).
4. Don't bitch if you don't get many pictures. Get a better waifu.
You can NOT register more than one waifu/husbando. You may change them by doing WaifuRegister again.

Frequently asked questions

Q: Why am I limited?
A: Limits are here for many reasons. If you want more "mah waifu" go google their name. MyWaifu is for a small handful of images at a time. Not a whole website worth of images in 10 minutes.
Q: What are the limits?
A: I'm not too sure on what to stick with so they're changing around daily for now. As I'm writing this the limit for MyWaifu (which includes MyHusbando), is limited to 6 every 4 hours (* while the waifuwednesday job is running this is limited to 2 for 1 hour, then moved back to 6 every 4).
WaifuRegister is limited to 5 every 72 hours.
Other commands such as "waifu" and "imouto" are at a default 10 every 2 hours.
Q: What time and how long is the WaifuWednesday job?
A: The job starts at 2pm (GMT), and runs for about 2 hours.
Q: Why such a long wait on WaifuRegister limit?
A: Stop being a slut and stick with one waifu. It's to stop you from abuseing the system and using waifuregister for any image you want. Once again: go to google for that.
Q: But I messed up on registering :(
A: That isn't a question. Stop fucking up.
Q: I was blocked!!! WHY!?!
A: I only block people that abuse the bot (this includes: finding and abuseing loopholes, wasting my servers cpu cycles on you trying to register a real person). You can also get blocked if you keep following and unfollowing to use waifuregister (you get 2 warnings).
Q: Can I register more than 1 waifu?
A: It is kinda planned (2 max), but I'm not sure how to implement this with keeping WaifuWednesday in mind (tweeting both would hit a twitter limit).
Q: How can I request a feature?

Q: I have a problem that isn't listed here!
