HusbandoRegister		 Register your waifu or husbando!
BEFORE REGISTERING READ THIS:
1. You MUST follow the bot to register!
2. Use their full name (If you're not sure check MyAnimeList)!
3. Don't include any special characters!
(ū > uu | ç > c | è > e)
4. PLEASE look at the examples if you are not sure.
5. If the name is single you can try adding the series name
they are from in brackets () after their name (read 4.).
6. Seasonal waifus are a bad idea as not much art is out there!
7. If the name is single, the bot will try and help you with a paste!
8. If the name is only single (i.e. C.C.) add "$" on the end (read 4.)!

DO NOT REGISTER: a real person, anime name,
your twitter handle, tags (1girl, 1boy, etc.), a pony,
or a full name as a single. 		"WaifuRegister Tojo Nozomi"close
